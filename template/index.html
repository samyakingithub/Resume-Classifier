Who are we?


In today's age the number of jobs and candidates have been on a rise. This makes it difficult for recruiters to link up with people who are perfect for their postings and likewise for students to find the job that fits their skillset.

What if we automated the linking of students and recruiters? The AI model used in our application uses NLP to identify key skills from student resumes and link them up with the most suitable job posting on our website!
